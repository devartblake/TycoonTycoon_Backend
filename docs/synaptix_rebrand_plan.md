
# SYNAPTIX REBRAND MASTER PLAN
## (Formerly Trivia Tycoon)

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Synaptix is a cognitive competition platform built on trivia, skill progression, and Theory of Mind systems. This document outlines branding, UX, architecture, and implementation strategy.

---

## 2. Naming Strategy
- Primary Brand: Synaptix
- Backup: Mindara
- Avoid: Trivia Tycoon (weak trademark)

### Rationale
- Synaptix derives from “synapse”
- Represents learning, intelligence, and neural growth
